# Notavex Agency Blueprint

## Positioning

Notavex is an AI automation agency for Quebec notarial offices. The agency helps notaries reduce administrative workload, improve client communication, and modernize real estate and estate-planning workflows without requiring them to replace their whole practice-management stack on day one.

## Core Promise

We design, implement, and support bilingual AI workflow systems built around Quebec notarial practice.

## Target Clients

- Solo notaries handling real estate, wills, mandates of protection, powers of attorney, and marriage contracts.
- Small notarial offices with high phone and email volume.
- Real estate-heavy offices managing mortgage mandates, lender reports, quittances, and mainlevees.
- Growth-minded offices that want automation but need hands-on implementation.

## Initial Service Offers

### 1. AI Front Office Setup

Includes Notavex Communicate workflows: NotaVoix, NotaMail, and Appointments.

- AI receptionist call flow design.
- Segregated incoming and outgoing call logs with caller name, phone number, email address, call time, transcription, summary, and contact creation.
- Succession call tracking with quarter-hour billing increments.
- Call-to-email conversion for follow-up messages and document checklists.
- Appointment booking automation.
- Appointment schedule tracking with confirmation, reminder, and missing-document status.
- Gmail or Outlook-connected mailbox inside Notavex for reading, drafting, sending, scheduling, labeling, and logging client emails while preserving the office's original inbox.
- WhatsApp Business channel for international clients, managed in the same unified inbox with prepared replies and client-file logging.
- AI email sorting by notarial category: real estate, testament and mandate, quittance/mainlevee follow-up, copies/declarations/invitations, marriage et contrat de marriage, consultation juridique, and succession.
- Intake questions by service type.
- Document checklist emails.
- Escalation rules for legal questions, complaints, urgency, and sensitive files.
- Call summaries, contact compilation, and inbox triage.

### 2. Notarial Workflow Dashboard

Includes NotaOffice style dashboards:

- Matter status board.
- Missing document tracking.
- Appointment and deadline visibility.
- Client communication log.
- Staff task assignment.
- Simple reporting for open files and bottlenecks.

### 3. Notavex Transaction Automation Sprint

Includes Notavex Transaction workflows, with NotaRad as the first module and NotaMarriage for marriage contracts and ceremonies:

- Quittance and mainlevee workflow mapping.
- Rapport preliminaire and rapport final checklist automation.
- Marriage contract intake, drafting status, review scheduling, ceremony tracking, and required-document follow-up.
- Lender-specific status tracking.
- Registry confirmation task flow mockup.
- Trust-account payment notification logic mockup.

## Demonstration Products

### Demo 1: Notavex Communicate / NotaVoix

An AI receptionist answers a call in French, identifies the caller's need, books a real estate appointment, collects contact details, sends a document checklist, and updates the appointment schedule.

### Demo 2: Notavex Communicate / NotaMail

An AI inbox assistant behaves like a full connected mailbox. It syncs with Gmail or Outlook, classifies emails, drafts replies, sends messages through the original account, proposes appointment slots, sends intake checklists, flags urgent legal questions, and creates follow-up tasks.

### Demo 3: Notavex Communicate / Appointments

A calendar view shows booked appointments, confirmation status, missing documents, available slots, and appointment drill-down details.

### Demo 4: NotaOffice

A practice dashboard shows active matters, missing documents, deadlines, calendar items, scheduled appointments, booking status, and urgent tasks.

### Demo 5: NotaRad

A discharge workflow shows mandate acceptance, certified statement request, draft quittance/mainlevee, registry submission, confirmation, and ProCardex payment-ready status.

### Demo 6: NotaRad / NotaRapport Subsection

A NotaRad subsection shows preliminary report preparation, missing insurance/title flags, notary review, signature, transmission, and final report status.

## Go-To-Market Path

1. Build the clickable demo.
2. Prepare a 7-minute bilingual sales script.
3. Interview 10 Quebec notaries or legal technicians.
4. Offer 3 pilot implementations at a discounted fixed price.
5. Turn repeated workflows into productized implementation packages.
6. Use pilot results to decide which module becomes the first true SaaS product.
