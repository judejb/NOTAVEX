# Notavex Demo Script

## Opening

Bonjour, merci de prendre le temps. Notavex helps Quebec notarial offices automate repetitive communication and file administration, especially around client intake, document collection, real estate files, and lender reporting.

This is not meant to replace the notary's professional judgment. The system handles routine administrative work and escalates anything that requires legal analysis, discretion, or human review.

## Demo Flow

### 1. NotaOffice Dashboard

Start with the dashboard.

Say:

"This is the control center for the office. At a glance, the notary and the technician can see active files, missing documents, upcoming deadlines, urgent items, and where each matter is blocked."

Point out:

- Active real estate file.
- Missing insurance confirmation.
- Quittance awaiting registry confirmation.
- Rapport preliminaire ready for review.
- AI email draft awaiting approval.

### 2. Notavex Communicate: NotaVoix

Open the AI receptionist panel.

Say:

"Here is the front-office automation. When a client calls, the AI assistant answers in French by default, can switch to English, books appointments, explains document requirements, and escalates legal questions."

Sample call:

"Bonjour, vous avez joint l'etude de Me Tremblay. Je suis l'assistant virtuel de l'etude. Comment puis-je vous aider aujourd'hui?"

Expected outcome:

- Caller name, phone number, and email address captured at pickup.
- Call time, direction, transcript, and summary logged.
- Incoming and outgoing calls are segregated for easier review.
- Succession outgoing calls tracked in quarter-hour billing increments.
- Client contact record created or updated.
- Call can be converted into a follow-up email draft.
- Appointment booked.
- Client details collected.
- Checklist triggered.
- Appointment schedule updated.
- Call summary attached to file.

### 3. Notavex Communicate: NotaMail

Open the inbox panel.

Say:

"The same logic applies to email. Routine emails can be drafted automatically. Sensitive emails are flagged for human review. The notary keeps control."

Point out:

- Gmail remains the original mailbox and system of record.
- WhatsApp Business can be connected for international clients, with conversations managed inside the same Notavex inbox.
- Notavex provides a full mailbox view for inbox, sent, drafts, scheduled, escalated, and archived emails.
- AI sorting creates notarial sections for real estate, testament and mandate, quittance/mainlevee follow-up, copies/declarations/invitations, marriage et contrat de marriage, legal consultations, and succession.
- Appointment request marked autonomous.
- Legal question marked escalation.
- Missing document reminder drafted.
- File status inquiry prepared for review.
- Emails can be composed and sent through the connected Gmail account from inside Notavex.

### 4. Notavex Communicate: Appointments

Open the appointments panel.

Say:

"Notavex is not only answering messages. It also books appointments, tracks whether the client confirmed, shows which documents are still missing, and schedules reminders before the appointment."

Point out:

- Today's appointment schedule.
- Confirmed, missing-document, staff-task, and tracked booking statuses.
- Available time slots.
- Booking form for a new client request.
- Calendar invite, SMS confirmation, document checklist, and reminder tracking.

### 5. NotaRad

Open the quittance/mainlevee workflow.

Say:

"For real estate-heavy offices, NotaRad demonstrates how a quittance or mainlevee file could be tracked from mandate reception through registry confirmation and payment readiness."

Point out:

- Mandate accepted.
- Certified mortgage statement received.
- Draft generated.
- Registry submitted.
- Dual confirmation required before payment notification.

### 6. NotaRad Subsection: NotaRapport

Stay inside NotaRad and open the lender report subsection.

Say:

"NotaRapport is a subsection of NotaRad focused on lender reporting. The system gathers mandate data, validates required fields, flags missing insurance or certificate issues, prepares the preliminary report, and later prepares the final report."

Point out:

- Rapport preliminaire ready.
- Insurance policy missing.
- Certificat de localisation flagged.
- Final report pending publication number.

## Close

The first step is not a full platform migration. We begin with one office workflow: phones and emails, intake, document collection, or real estate tracking. We map the office's actual process, build the automation, test it with staff, and keep human approval wherever professional judgment is required.

## Discovery Questions

- Which task consumes the most staff time each week?
- How many calls and emails does the office handle per day?
- Which files are most often delayed by missing documents?
- Who currently tracks lender reports and registry confirmations?
- Which tools are already in place: Outlook, Gmail, ProCardex, Para-Maitre, Pronotaire, Assyst Immobilier?
- What should always be reviewed by a notary before anything is sent?
